Document Camera

Mom needed something to show papers and books on large screens for her classroom. I suggested she used a webcam, but then I put my thinking cap on and decided to design a functional document cam using an existing webcam! The design utilizes a balanced-arm mechanism (from a mic boom), a logitech webcam, and an LED strip controlled by an Arduino microcontroller. I also designed and printed an enclosure for the electronics to make operation easier for my mom!
