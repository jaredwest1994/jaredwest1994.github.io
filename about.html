About Me!

Personal Life

I was born and raised in the small town of Burns Lake in Northern British Columbia.

Education

In Burns Lake, I attended the local high school Lakes District Secondary School, and then moved to Prince George to attend post secondary at UNBC. I took three years of general studies to figure out what I wanted to do, during one of those years I was told to look into engineering and I haven't looked back since. Following my three years at UNBC, I moved down the hill to CNC where I did an upgrading year, followed by my first year of engineering. The next step was to move to Vancouver to complete my bachelors at UBC! I completed my Bachelors of Applied Science in Electrical Engineering in 2020.
