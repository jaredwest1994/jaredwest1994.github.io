Norse Creek Falls

Amy and I walked from Colleymount Road because of the unplowed access road. We followed the road along an existing snowshoe trail. After the 1.5km hike, we reached the trailhead for a banana break and to take in the view. The trail itself hadn't been touched by anyone, so we broke the trail. The trail follows the ridge along Norse Creek until the waterfalls can be seen.
